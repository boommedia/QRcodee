Core Value Drivers
🔄
Dynamic QR = Zero Reprint Cost
Static QR codes are permanent — change the destination and you reprint everything. Dynamic QR codes let you point the same printed code to any URL, anytime. Change a menu link, seasonal promotion, or event page without touching the physical print.
↓ 100% reprint cost reduction
📊
Scan Analytics = Offline Attribution
Every scan is logged: timestamp, city, device, OS, browser. For the first time, offline marketing channels (flyers, packaging, signage, menus) have real attribution data. Businesses can see which physical touchpoint drives the most engagement.
Full offline-to-online attribution
🎨
Custom Design Without a Designer
Brand-colored QR codes with logo overlays, custom dot shapes, and styled frames — in 60 seconds. Competitors charge $50–$150/hr for this. QRcodee's visual editor puts it in the hands of the business owner directly, with no design skill required.
On-brand QRs in <60 seconds
⚡
Bulk Generation = Agency Superpower
Upload a CSV of 500 URLs, get 500 custom-designed QR codes in a ZIP file. Menu items, product SKUs, event tables, location pages — anything that needs a unique QR at scale. What took hours of copy-paste is done in seconds.
500 QRs in <30 seconds
