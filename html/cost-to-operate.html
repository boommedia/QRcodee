⚡
$0 QR Generation Cost — Structural Cost Advantage
Unlike SaaS tools that pay per QR code generated via third-party APIs ($0.01–$0.05 each), QRcodee uses qr-code-styling (open-source, MIT license) running server-side on Vercel. At 10,000 QR codes/month, competitors pay ~$200–$500 in API fees. QRcodee pays $0. This compounds to a significant structural margin advantage as volume scales.
